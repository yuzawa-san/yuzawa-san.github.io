See You Later: Privacy and Terms of Service

This application allows users to affirmatively share maps of their planned activities and estimated times of arrival with specific other persons via the Apple Messages System (iMessage). These messages are stored in the iMessage ecosystem. The saved history of routes and appearances are saved in the local preference storage on the device. No data is stored elsewhere. Deleting the app will delete the data from your device.

This is a personal project.
